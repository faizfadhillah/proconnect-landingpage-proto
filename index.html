Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is a "seat"?

A seat is a paid license for one person who needs to post jobs and manage hiring. Everyone else in your company can be invited as a Member Profile for FREE to view the directory and apply for internal jobs.

Can I mix Full Recruiter and Department Head seats?

Yes — most enterprise customers mix both. Full seats for HR leadership, Department seats for hiring managers who only need their own team's pipeline.

Why are prices different in each country?

We price in local currency and adjust to local purchasing power so operators in every ASEAN market have equal access.

Do I get charged per branch or location?

No. Pricing is strictly per seat — not per branch, location, or job posting.

Our Mission

To improve workforce mobility and hiring quality in the hospitality sector by providing verified, skill-based job matching tools that benefit candidates, employers, and education institutions.

Across ASEAN, the hospitality sector employs 15 million+ people. Yet hiring remains fragmented, expensive, and riddled with credential fraud. Recruiting agents charge $7,000–$12,000 per hire while schools spend up to $2.5M annually on placement services. ProConnect was built to fix this.
