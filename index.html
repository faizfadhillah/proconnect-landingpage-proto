Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is a "seat"?

A seat is a paid license for one person who needs to post jobs and manage hiring. Everyone else in your company can be invited as a Member Profile for FREE to view the directory and apply for internal jobs.

Can I mix Full Recruiter and Department Head seats?

Yes. Most enterprise customers use a combination — Full seats for HR leadership and Department seats for hiring managers who only need their own department's pipeline.

Why are prices different in each country?

We price in local currency and adjust to local purchasing power so small operators in Laos or Cambodia have the same access to tools as enterprises in Singapore.

Do I get charged per branch or location?

No. Pricing is strictly per seat — not per branch, location, or job posting. Post unlimited jobs across unlimited branches on any paid plan.

How does credential verification work?

Partner schools confirm diplomas directly in the platform. Skills are assessed against the ASEAN-MRA-TP competency framework. Employers see the verified badge on every profile — no manual checks.
