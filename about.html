Our Mission

Building the Future of
Hospitality Hiring in ASEAN

ProConnect is the regional platform for hospitality talent — endorsed by ASEANTA and partnered with national tourism ministries across Southeast Asia.

Our Mission

To improve workforce mobility across Southeast Asia.

ASEAN is home to 15 million+ people employed in hospitality — but the industry is plagued by fragmented hiring, unverifiable credentials, and costly cross-border placement.

Hotels and F&B groups spend $7,000–$12,000 per hire and up to $2.5M annually on external placement services. ProConnect replaces that with a single, verified, regional talent pool.
